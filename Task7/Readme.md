Insights from Global Superstore Dashboard:

1-Technology emerges as the highest-revenue generating category, indicating strong demand for tech products compared to Furniture and Office Supplies.

2-The West region records the highest total sales, showing better market penetration and customer base than other regions.

3-Profit trends over time show overall growth with noticeable fluctuations, suggesting seasonal demand and varying operational costs across years.

4-Despite high sales volume, certain categories and regions contribute lower profit margins, highlighting the impact of discounts and cost structures.

5-Profit Margin KPI reveals that increased sales do not always translate into proportional profit growth, emphasizing the need for margin-focused strategies.

6-Segment-wise filtering shows that the Consumer segment contributes the largest share of revenue, making it the most important customer segment for business focus.

7-Interactive filters demonstrate that regional and segment-based performance varies significantly, reinforcing the importance of targeted marketing strategies.

8-The dashboard enables management to quickly identify high-performing areas and underperforming segments, supporting data-driven decision-making.
